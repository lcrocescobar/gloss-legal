2. How your photos are handled

This is the part of the Service people ask about most, so we want to be precise:

  1. When you take a scan, your photo is resized on your device and transmitted to our server over an encrypted connection (TLS).
  2. The photo is held in memory only for the duration of the analysis request, passed to our AI provider to produce your scores, and then discarded. The photo is not saved to our servers, not written to our database, not logged, and not used to train AI models by us.
  3. A small thumbnail of your scan may be stored only on your own device so you can see your personal timeline. It never leaves your device, and deleting the App deletes it.
  4. Only the resulting numbers and short text (scores and observations) are stored in our database, associated with your account, so your history can survive a reinstall or a new phone.

3. How we use your information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide the core Service: analyze your scans, compute your scores, build your personalized routine, and show your progress over time;
  • Maintain your account and sync your scan history across devices;
  • Personalize the analysis using your skin profile and recent score history;
  • Process and manage subscriptions, trials, and entitlements;
  • Schedule the local reminders you ask for (reminders are generated on your device);
  • Enforce fair-use limits, prevent fraud and abuse, and protect the security and integrity of the Service;
  • Respond to your support requests;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles about you.

4. AI processing

Skin scans are analyzed by an artificial-intelligence vision model operated by Anthropic, PBC. Your photo and relevant, pseudonymous parts of your skin profile are submitted to Anthropic's API for the sole purpose of generating your scores and observations, subject to Anthropic's commercial API terms, under which API inputs and outputs are not used to train Anthropic's models without customer opt-in. AI outputs are estimates generated by a statistical model: they can be imperfect, may vary between scans, and are provided for general informational and cosmetic-tracking purposes only (see Section 13).

7. Data retention

  • Scan photos: not retained — processed and discarded within the analysis request (see Section 2).
  • Scan scores, observations, and skin profile: retained while your account exists, so your history is available to you.
  • Usage/abuse-prevention records: retained for a limited period consistent with fraud-prevention needs.
  • Account data: retained until you delete your account.
  • Support emails: retained as long as needed to resolve your issue and for our records.

When you delete your account (Settings → "Delete account & data"), your account, profile, scan history, and usage records are permanently deleted from our production systems, and data stored on your device is cleared. Residual copies may persist for a limited time in encrypted backups before being purged in the ordinary course.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including: encryption of data in transit (TLS) for all communication between the App and our servers; encryption at rest for our database; row-level access controls so each account can only ever read its own data; server-side (not client-side) enforcement of access and usage rules; and secret credentials stored in a managed secrets system rather than in the App. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security; you use the Service at your own risk to the extent permitted by law. If we learn of a breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

9. Your rights and choices

  • Access & portability: you can view your scan history in the App at any time; you may request a copy of your stored personal data by contacting us.
  • Correction: you can update your skin profile in the App; contact us to correct account information.
  • Deletion: the App includes full self-service deletion (Settings → "Delete account & data"). You may also request deletion by email.
  • Withdraw consent: stop taking scans, or delete your account, at any time.
  • Notifications: reminders are optional and controlled in the App and in iOS Settings.
  • Objection/restriction (EEA/UK): you may object to or request restriction of certain processing; contact us.
  • Complaints: EEA/UK users may lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority; we would appreciate the chance to address your concern first.

We will respond to verifiable requests within the timeframes required by applicable law. We may need to verify your identity (for example, by confirming control of the email on the account) before acting on a request.
